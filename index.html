Established in 1914 to represent stagehands and technicians in Savannah, Statesboro, Hilton Head Island and Coastal Georgia, we are a mixed Local handling Corporate, Industrial, Pipe & Drape, Exposition, Theatrical and Live Concert Production, and are proud members of the AFL-CIO and the Central Labor Council.

Our mission is to provide quality representation for workers and top-notch technical labor for productions. Contact us today to get involved!

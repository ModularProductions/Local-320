Calendar of Events

Regular meetings at our Hall are generally held at 7pm on the second Tuesday of the month and are open to all members in good standing. Rescheduled or relocated meetings will be announced in advance. Remaining in good standing and attending meetings allows members to vote on matters at hand and have a voice in their Union. (Note: The hall is between 31st and 32nd on Paulsen St; we enter in the back of the building on Vine St.)

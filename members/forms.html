Forms

The following are standard hiring forms required by the Federal Government and the State of Georgia. We have made these available in the event that you have worked or will be working with IATSE Local 320. You will need to complete this necessary tax information and fill out our wage assessment form before you can be hired, and ultimately paid, by a production company that has contracted for Local 320 to supply referral labor. All forms are in Abode .PDF format. If you do not have Adobe Acrobat you can download Acrobat Reader for free here.

New Hires

New hires (those not already in our payroll system) should print, fill out, and deliver to the Job Steward or Business Agent the following forms. Ideally, you should have them completed and ready before the job begins. If these forms must be delivered via envelope, send them to our MAILING address and notify your Job Steward or an Officer to be on the lookout for them.

  • 2014 Form I-9
  • 2014 Form W-4
  • Form G-4 (not necessary for most employers, but it never hurts to have one ready)
  • Local 320 Wage Assessment Check-Off
  • Along with the above forms you will need to provide originals or photocopies of either your PASSPORT -or- a combination of your DRIVER'S LICENSE (or State ID) AND your SOCIAL SECURITY CARD (or Birth Certificate).

In addition to the the above forms, certain venues require additional paperwork.

  • Savannah Civic Center Hiring Info
  • Lucas Theater for the Arts Assessment Check-Off

You may also find it convenient to enlist in our payroll company's Direct Deposit.

  • UTP Direct Deposit Application

Stewards

You should have in your kit a minimum of the following:

  • 4 x 2014 Form I-9
  • 4 x 2014 Form W-4
  • 4 x Form G-4 (not necessary for most employers)
  • 4 x Local 320 Wage Assessment Check-Off
  • 4 x Blank Sign-In Sheet and Steward Report (download and edit Excel file here)
  • 4 x Accident/Injury Report Form
  • A couple of pens

If you are the steward on a call you are responsible for getting new hires to fill out all necessary forms and for PROMPTLY turning those forms along with your Steward Reports in to the Business Agent for invoicing.
